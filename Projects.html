Currently I'm working on Renewing Race Street-A Mobility Analysis- Phialdelphia, PA.This study evaluates a proposed design for Race Street between 9th and fifth Streets by reducing the north and south crossing distance at most intersections; change the road to a three travel lane configuration at its maximum width; and add protected bicycle, plantings, and pedestrian amenities. The design would have minimal impact on traffic operations while creating a significantly safer traffic environment.
