About Me

I was born in Hyderabad, India on December 31st. I graduated with a bachelor's in Urban Planning from School of Planning and Architecture, Jawaharlal Nehru Architecture and Fine Arts University in 2010. While pursuing my graduation I attended Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Karlsruhe, Germany for a student exchange program unde DAAD scholarship, awarded by Federal Government of Germany to promote cross cultural experiences for students from developing countries.


I was awarded the DAAD Scholarship. I began work at Google India Private Limited as a GIS Analyst from 2010 to 2011.In 2011 December I initiated a start up project with a group of friends and successfully completed Slum Free City Plan for Bhilai Municipal Corporation. Slum Free City Plan of Action is envisioned for a period of 20 years to eradicate slums in all metros and major cities in India. It's an initiative of Ministry of Housing and Urban Poverty Alleviation .
